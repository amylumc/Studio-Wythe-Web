Who We Are


It all starts with Ashley McClure, our lead Architect. Ashley graduated from Auburn University in 2004 with dual degrees in Architecture and Interior Architecture. Ashley has worked in both large and small firms over the last 11 years, honing his skills as a designer as well as a project manager, hoping along the way to build his own space in the industry. Hence, Studio Wythe. We are a full service design firm specializing in project types that focuses on the human experience: residential, restaurant, and retail. We live, work and play in Decatur/Atlanta Georgia. We offer a tailored approach to design that responds and innovatively collaborates with the creative energy of our clients. We focus on creating evocative spaces that enhance the human condition.

Why Wythe?

A wythe is a single layer of brick, especially in older buildings where the entire structure was multiple layers of brick thick. These layers were tied together with even more bricks laid perpendicular to the layers, which is the origination of most masonry traditional coursing patterns. These interlocking and weaving layers brought stability and strength, and it’s why so many of these structures are still standing today.
